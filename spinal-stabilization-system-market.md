<p><p>The spinal stabilization system market includes various types designed to enhance spinal support and mobility. Posterior interspinous spacers are used to maintain space between vertebrae, relieving pressure on spinal nerves. Pedicle screws anchor spinal instrumentation to the vertebral body, providing robust stabilization for fusion procedures. Total facet replacement devices aim to restore motion and stability by replacing damaged facet joints, optimizing spinal alignment. These innovations are pivotal in addressing spinal disorders and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
