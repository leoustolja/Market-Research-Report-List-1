<p><strong>The CVD Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PECVD</li><li>MOCVD</li><li>ThermalCVD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CVD equipment encompasses various techniques used for depositing thin films and coatings on substrates. Plasma-Enhanced Chemical Vapor Deposition (PECVD) employs plasma to enhance chemical reactions at lower temperatures, ideal for flexible substrates. Metal-Organic Chemical Vapor Deposition (MOCVD) utilizes metal-organic precursors to create high-quality semiconductor materials, crucial for optoelectronic devices. Thermal CVD relies on thermal energy to drive chemical reactions, suitable for high-temperature applications. Each type serves distinct industries, including electronics, solar energy, and advanced materials manufacturing.</p></p>
