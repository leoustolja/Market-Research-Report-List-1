<p><p>The neoprene market comprises several grades tailored for specific applications. Normal linear grades are standard types used in general applications, offering flexibility and durability. Precrosslinked grades enhance heat resistance and mechanical strength, ideal for demanding environments. Sulfur modified grades provide improved weathering resistance and superior performance in automotive and construction industries. Slow crystallizing grades are designed for low-temperature applications, ensuring flexibility and resilience under cooler conditions. Each grade serves distinct needs, contributing to neoprene's versatility across various industries.</p></p>
