<p><p>The explosive detectors market comprises various types, each utilizing distinct technologies for detecting explosives. X-ray explosive detectors analyze the density and atomic number of materials to identify suspicious items in luggage. MRI explosive detectors leverage magnetic resonance imaging to discern different substances. Steam explosive detectors utilize steam to detect explosive compounds through chemical reaction. Lastly, neutron explosive detectors employ neutron radiation to identify explosives based on their interaction with certain materials, providing comprehensive security solutions in diverse environments.</p></p>
