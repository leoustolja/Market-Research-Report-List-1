<p><p>Calibration Management Systems can be categorized into two main market types: installed and cloud-based. Installed systems are software solutions that reside on local servers, offering greater control and customization but requiring significant IT resources for maintenance and updates. In contrast, cloud-based systems are hosted on external servers, providing easier access and lower upfront costs while facilitating automatic updates and scalability. Both types serve the same purpose—ensuring accurate measurement and compliance—but cater to different organizational needs and infrastructure capabilities.</p></p>
