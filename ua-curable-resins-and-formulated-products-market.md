<p><p>The UA (Ultraviolet-A) Curable Resins and Formulated Products market encompasses various segments including oligomers, monomers, photoinitiators, additives, and others. Oligomers are pre-polymers that provide backbone structure and functionality, while monomers are reactive compounds that facilitate polymerization. Photoinitiators trigger the curing process upon exposure to UV light, enabling hardening. Additives enhance properties like fluidity, adhesion, and flexibility. The "Others" category includes specialty products catering to specific applications, collectively driving innovation in coatings, adhesives, and inks.</p></p>
