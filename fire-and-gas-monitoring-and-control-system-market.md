<p><p>Fire and Gas Monitoring and Control Systems are essential for ensuring safety in industries. The Fixed Type system is stationary, using fixed sensors installed in specific locations to detect gases and flames, ideal for high-risk areas. In contrast, the Distributed Type system consists of multiple interconnected sensors spread throughout the facility, providing comprehensive coverage and better flexibility. Both types enhance safety, but their applications vary based on the layout and specific safety requirements of the facility.</p></p>
