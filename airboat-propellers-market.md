<p><strong>The Airboat Propellers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Drive</li><li>Reduction Gear</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The airboat propellers market features two primary types: direct drive and reduction gear propellers. Direct drive propellers connect directly to the engine, providing simplicity and a lightweight design, making them suitable for smaller airboats. In contrast, reduction gear propellers use a gearbox to reduce engine RPMs, allowing for larger blades and improved efficiency at higher speeds. This type is often preferred for larger vessels, as it enhances thrust and performance, catering to diverse boating needs and conditions.</p></p>
