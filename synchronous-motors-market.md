<p><p>Synchronous motors are categorized into two main market types: horizontal and vertical. Horizontal synchronous motors are typically used in industrial applications, featuring a shaft oriented parallel to the ground, ideal for driving pumps, compressors, and fans. In contrast, vertical synchronous motors have their shafts positioned upright, making them suitable for space-constrained environments and specific applications like water treatment and mining. Both types are designed for high efficiency and precise speed control, serving diverse industrial needs effectively.</p></p>
