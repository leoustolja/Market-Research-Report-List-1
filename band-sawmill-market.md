<p><ul><li>Horizontal</li><li>Vertical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The band sawmill market can be classified into horizontal and vertical types based on the positioning of the saw blade during operation. Horizontal band sawmills feature a horizontally mounted blade, allowing for efficient cutting of large logs into slabs, making them ideal for high-volume production. In contrast, vertical band sawmills position the blade vertically, enabling more precise cuts and intricate shapes, which is suitable for smaller operations and specialty wood products. Both types cater to different industrial needs and applications.</p></p>
