<p><strong>The Cuffed Endotracheal Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Pressure-Low Volume Endotracheal Tube</li><li>Low Pressure-High Volume Endotracheal Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cuffed endotracheal tube market is categorized into high pressure-low volume and low pressure-high volume tubes. High pressure-low volume tubes create a tight seal in the trachea with minimal cuff volume, suitable for positive pressure ventilation but posing a risk of tracheal injury. In contrast, low pressure-high volume tubes distribute pressure over a larger surface area, minimizing trauma to the trachea while providing effective ventilation. Each type is designed for specific clinical applications, ensuring patient safety and optimal respiratory management.</p></p>
