<p><p>The Content Delivery Network (CDN) service market is divided into two main types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based CDNs leverage a distributed network of servers hosted online, allowing companies to deliver content efficiently without extensive infrastructure investment. This model offers scalability and flexibility. In contrast, on-premise CDNs involve deploying servers within an organization’s own infrastructure, providing greater control over content delivery but requiring significant upfront costs and maintenance. Each type caters to different business needs and resource availability.</p></p>
