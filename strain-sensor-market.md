<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Strain Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Strain Sensor</li><li>Transverse Strain Sensor</li><li>Longitudinal Strain Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The strain sensor market is categorized into three main types: Surface Strain Sensors, which measure strain directly on surfaces of materials to assess structural integrity; Transverse Strain Sensors, designed to detect strain perpendicular to the applied load, important for evaluating material behavior under different stresses; and Longitudinal Strain Sensors, which measure strain along the direction of the applied load, crucial for applications in construction and manufacturing. Each type serves specific applications in monitoring, testing, and structural analysis.</p></p>
