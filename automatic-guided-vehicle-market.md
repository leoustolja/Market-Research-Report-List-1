<p><p>The Automatic Guided Vehicle (AGV) market comprises three main types: Unit Load AGVs, Automated Forklift AGVs, and Tugger AGVs. Unit Load AGVs are designed to transport bulk items or pallets, enhancing efficiency in warehouses. Automated Forklift AGVs handle material lifting and transport, providing flexibility in dynamic environments. Tugger AGVs, on the other hand, are used to pull carts or trailers, often for assembly lines or distribution centers, optimizing material flow and reducing manual labor requirements across various industries.</p></p>
