<p><p>Air suspension is a type of vehicle suspension system that utilizes compressed air to provide a smooth ride and adjustable vehicle height. <p><p>The air suspension market is primarily divided into two types: electronically controlled and non-electronically controlled systems. Electronically controlled air suspension systems utilize advanced sensors and computer algorithms to automatically adjust the air pressure in the suspension, enhancing ride comfort and stability. In contrast, non-electronically controlled air suspension systems rely on a more traditional mechanical approach, manually adjusting air pressure to stabilize the vehicle. Both types cater to varied consumer preferences, performance requirements, and cost considerations in automotive applications.</p></p>
