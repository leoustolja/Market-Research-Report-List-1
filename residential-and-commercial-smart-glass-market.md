<p><p>The residential and commercial smart glass market features three main types: electrochromic, thermochromic, and photochromic glass. Electrochromic glass changes tint in response to electrical stimuli, providing energy savings and privacy control. Thermochromic glass adjusts its opacity with temperature variations, enhancing comfort and reducing heating and cooling costs. Photochromic glass automatically darkens in sunlight, offering UV protection and glare reduction. Together, these technologies enhance energy efficiency, aesthetics, and occupant comfort in both residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
