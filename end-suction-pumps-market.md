<p><strong>The End Suction Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Frame Mounted End Suction Pump</li><li>Close Coupled End Suction Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>End suction pumps are categorized into two main types: frame mounted and close coupled. Frame mounted end suction pumps are installed on a distinct base, allowing for flexibility in configuration and easier maintenance, as they often house larger motors. Close coupled end suction pumps, in contrast, integrate the pump and motor into a single unit, minimizing space requirements and simplifying installation. Both types find applications across various industries for moving fluids, each offering unique advantages in operational efficiency and layout.</p></p>
