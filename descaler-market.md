<p><p>The descaler market is segmented into three main types: gel, liquid, and powder. Gel descalers provide targeted application, effectively clinging to surfaces for thorough cleaning, making them ideal for vertical or hard-to-reach areas. Liquid descalers offer ease of use and quick dispersal, suitable for general maintenance in appliances. Powder descalers, often cost-effective, require activation with water, making them versatile for both household and industrial applications. Each type caters to specific needs based on convenience, effectiveness, and application method.</p></p>
