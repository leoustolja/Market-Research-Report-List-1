<p><p>Supercritical Fluid Chromatography (SFC) is a powerful analytical technique that utilizes supercritical fluids, primarily carbon dioxide, as the mobile phase for the separation and analysis of compounds in pharmaceuticals. The trend towards integrating SFC with other techniques, such as mass spectrometry, is also enhancing the capabilities of pharmaceutical analytics, leading to broader application areas.
