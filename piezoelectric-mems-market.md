<p><p>The Piezoelectric MEMS market can be categorized into three approaches: </p><p>1. **Additive Approach** focuses on 3D printing techniques to create complex piezoelectric structures, allowing for customization and reduced material waste.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Subtractive Approach** involves traditional machining and etching processes to remove material from a substrate, enabling precise and detailed designs, but often resulting in more waste.</p><p>3. **Integrative Approach** combines both methods, allowing for seamless integration of piezoelectric components with electronic circuits, enhancing performance and minimizing assembly complexity in MEMS devices.</p></p>
