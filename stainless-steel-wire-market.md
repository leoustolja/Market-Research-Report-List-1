<p><p>The stainless steel wire market is primarily categorized into Type 316, Type 304, and others. Type 316 wire contains molybdenum, enhancing its corrosion resistance, making it suitable for marine environments and chemical processing. Type 304 wire is the most common, known for its excellent oxidation resistance and formability, ideal for food processing and kitchen applications. Other types include specialty alloys designed for specific uses, such as high-strength applications or extreme temperatures, catering to diverse industrial sectors.</p></p>
