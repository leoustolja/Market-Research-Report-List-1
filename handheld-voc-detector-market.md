<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Handheld VOC Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photoionization Detector (PID)</li><li>Flame Ionization Detector (FID)</li><li>Metal Oxide Semiconductor Sensor (MOS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld VOC detector market comprises various technologies, including Photoionization Detectors (PIDs), Flame Ionization Detectors (FIDs), and Metal Oxide Semiconductor Sensors (MOS). PIDs use ultraviolet light to ionize volatile organic compounds, offering high sensitivity. FIDs combust gases to measure ionized particles, making them effective for hydrocarbons. MOS sensors utilize changes in resistance when exposed to VOCs, providing a cost-effective and compact option. Each type serves different applications based on sensitivity, response time, and operational environments.</p></p>
