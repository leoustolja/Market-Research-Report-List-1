<p><p>The powdered tea market is primarily categorized into two types: drinking-use tea and additive-use tea. Drinking-use tea consists of finely processed teas designed for direct consumption as beverages, providing convenience and quick preparation. In contrast, additive-use tea refers to powdered teas utilized as ingredients in food products, supplements, or flavor enhancers, enriching dishes and beverages. Each segment serves distinct consumer preferences, with drinking-use tea focusing on ready-to-drink experiences while additive-use tea enhances culinary applications.</p></p>
