<p><p>The transmission fluids market comprises three main types: mineral, synthetic, and semi-synthetic fluids. Mineral fluids are derived from refined crude oil, offering basic lubrication and protection. Synthetic fluids, engineered through chemical processes, provide superior performance, enhanced stability, and better temperature resistance, making them ideal for high-performance vehicles. Semi-synthetic fluids blend mineral and synthetic components, offering a balance of performance and cost-effectiveness. Each type serves distinct market needs, influencing vehicle efficiency and transmission longevity.</p></p>
