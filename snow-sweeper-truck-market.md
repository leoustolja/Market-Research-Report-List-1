<p><p>The snow sweeper truck market primarily features two main types: rotary snow sweeper trucks and wedge snow sweeper trucks. Rotary snow sweeper trucks utilize rotating brushes to efficiently clear snow from surfaces, making them ideal for larger areas and maintaining quick operational speeds. In contrast, wedge snow sweeper trucks employ a wedge-shaped blade that pushes snow aside, effectively managing compacted snow in urban settings where maneuverability is essential. Both types cater to different needs in snow management and road maintenance sectors.</p></p>
