<p><p>Thin-layer deposition technologies encompass various techniques critical for creating thin films in semiconductor manufacturing and other applications. Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) involves the chemical reaction of gaseous precursors to form solid films on substrates, valued for uniformity and scalability. Ion Implantation introduces ions into materials to alter their properties, ensuring precision in doping. Molecular Beam Epitaxy (MBE) allows for the growth of crystalline structures layer by layer under ultra-high vacuum, offering exceptional control over film composition and thickness. Together, these technologies drive advancements in electronics, optics, and materials science.</p></p>
