<p><strong>The High Frequency Relays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Mount High Frequency Relays</li><li>Through Hole High Frequency Relays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High Frequency Relays market is categorized into Surface Mount and Through Hole types. Surface Mount High Frequency Relays are designed for compact applications, offering efficient space utilization and ease of automated assembly, making them ideal for modern electronic devices. In contrast, Through Hole High Frequency Relays feature leads that pass through the PCB, providing robust mechanical stability and reliability, suitable for high-power applications. Both types serve critical roles in telecommunications, automotive, and industrial sectors, enhancing signal integrity and performance.</p></p>
