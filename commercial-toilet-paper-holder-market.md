<p><p>The commercial toilet paper holder market is primarily divided into two types: roll paper and folded paper holders. Roll paper holders are designed to accommodate standard toilet paper rolls, allowing for easy replacement and continuous supply. They are popular in high-traffic areas due to their efficiency and longer usage duration. Folded paper holders, on the other hand, dispense pre-folded paper sheets, offering users a more controlled amount of paper per use. This type is often found in restrooms where hygiene and waste reduction are prioritized.</p></p>
