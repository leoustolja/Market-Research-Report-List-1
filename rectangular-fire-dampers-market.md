<p><p>Rectangular fire dampers are essential components in HVAC systems designed to prevent the spread of smoke and flames in buildings. There are two main types: manual fire dampers and motorized fire dampers. Manual fire dampers require manual operation to close during a fire, providing a straightforward and low-maintenance solution. In contrast, motorized fire dampers are equipped with automated actuators that close the damper automatically in response to fire detection systems, ensuring rapid response and enhanced safety in emergency situations.</p></p>
