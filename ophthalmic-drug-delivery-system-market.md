<p><p>The ophthalmic drug delivery system market comprises various types, including implantable systems, particulate systems, and others. Implantable ophthalmic drug delivery systems involve surgically placed devices that release medication over extended periods, offering sustained treatment with reduced frequent dosing. Particulate ophthalmic drug delivery systems utilize nano or microparticles to enhance drug solubility and bioavailability, allowing for targeted and controlled release. Other systems may include traditional methods like eye drops or gels and emerging technologies that aim to improve the efficacy and patient compliance of ocular therapies. </p></p>
